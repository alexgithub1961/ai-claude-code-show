## Executive Summary

After running comprehensive GEO and SEO visibility assessments with 143 queries across multiple categories, we have definitive findings:

###  Critical Findings

1. **GEO Visibility**: ❌ **Zero** - Google AI Overview does not feature vendor names
2. **Traditional SEO**: ❌ **Minimal** - Only EPAM appears (2/25 queries)
3. **Competitive Gap**: ✅ **None** - Competitors also have low/zero visibility
4. **Pattern**: ✅ **Systematic** - Issue affects entire industry vertical

### Bottom Line

**This is not a First Line Software problem - it's an industry pattern.** Google intentionally avoids vendor recommendations in B2B software services queries, both in AI Overview (GEO) and to a large extent in organic results.

## Root Cause Analysis

### Why No AI Overview?

1. **Commercial Intent**: Google classifies these as commercial queries
2. **Liability Concerns**: AI vendor recommendations could be problematic
3. **Data Quality**: Limited training data for niche B2B services
4. **Policy Decision**: Intentional design choice by Google

### Why Low SEO Visibility?

1. **Competitive Market**: Thousands of similar companies globally
2. **Query Ambiguity**: "Consulting services" matches too broadly
3. **Geographic Factors**: Global results dilute regional players
4. **Content Gap**: Insufficient differentiated, indexed content
