## Key Insights

### 1. AI Overview Coverage Gap
**Finding**: Google AI Overview is **not designed** to answer queries about service providers and vendors in the software development/IT outsourcing space.

**Evidence**:
- 95%+ of queries returned "An AI Overview is not available for this search"
- Even broad, informational queries don't trigger AI Overview
- Query refinement strategies didn't improve AI Overview generation rates

### 2. Query Type Patterns

**Queries that DON'T trigger AI Overview**:
- "top software development companies in [location]"
- "best [service] companies"
- "[company] competitors"
- "companies like [competitor]"
- "[location] IT outsourcing companies"

**Queries that MIGHT get organic results**:
- "companies specializing in [technology]"
- "[technology] implementation services"

### 3. Industry-Specific Behavior
Google AI Overview appears to **avoid making vendor recommendations** in the B2B software services space, likely because:
- It's a commercial/transactional query space
- Recommendations could be seen as endorsements
- Information changes frequently
- High stakes for businesses

## Why GEO Visibility is Low

### Technical Reasons
1. **Query Intent**: Google classifies these as commercial queries, not informational
2. **AI Overview Design**: Designed for factual Q&A, not vendor recommendations
3. **Data Sources**: Google may lack quality training data for niche B2B vendors
4. **Liability**: AI-generated vendor recommendations could be problematic

### Market Reasons
1. **Fragmented Market**: Thousands of software development companies globally
2. **Regional Variations**: Different leaders in different markets
3. **Rapid Change**: Company reputations and capabilities evolve quickly
4. **Subjective Quality**: "Best" is context-dependent
